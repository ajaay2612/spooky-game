Test Patterns

What you should see on the monitor:

  • Full texture should fill the entire screen
  • Green border should be visible on all 4 sides
  • Text should be centered
  • No stretching or distortion

If you see issues:

  • Only left side visible: UV mapping is wrong (needs adjustment in Blender)
  • Stretched/squashed: Aspect ratio mismatch
  • Upside down: V coordinate needs flipping
